Incubation

You have an idea that has the potential to disrupt the status quo; we have the resources and know-how to turn that idea into a fully-functional product. Together we are partners in turning the vision into reality.

Longitude Innovation have a wealth of experience of working in start-ups, adopting agile project management practices to build a user-centric product that delivers product market fit. We're not an outsourced development team; we're your technology partners that stand side-by-side with you at every stage of the journey.

Innovation

Innovation does not have to always lead to headline-grabbing disruption; it could just be a collection of small, ongoing improvements that keep your customers engaged to defend your current market position. However, what is true about innovation is that without it, your business will be falling behind.

No matter what the ambition is, whether it be to defend, differentiate or disrupt, Longitude Innovation utilise a wide range of tools and frameworks to support SMEs to create and maintain a continuous cycle of innovation to support their growth ambitions.

Transformation

People, processes and technology are at the heart of every business; the combination and relationship between them will directly influence the value your business provides and ultimately how successful your strategy will be. In these increasingly technology and experience led times, Longitude Innovation help clients to navigate the maze of new technologies from Artificial Intelligence, to Blockchain, to Software Robots, to map their journey to sustainable competitive advantage and growth.

Acquisition

Whether you are looking to grow your company through the acquisiton of others, or feel it's time for you to hand over to someone else to nurture your company's future progression, Longitude Innovation have the team and network to deliver the value the transaction deserves.
